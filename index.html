Project Overview:

During my tenure as a Technical Lead at GAP Inc., I spearheaded multiple projects aimed at enhancing global operations, supporting critical business functions, and ensuring seamless integration across various systems. These projects encompassed development, production support, data integration, and application maintenance, each playing a crucial role in driving efficiency and innovation within GAP Inc.'s technology landscape.

Key Projects:

  • Global Assortment Support (PETE): Provided L2 support for a highly visual system that enabled merchants to manage global assortments, integrating with upstream and downstream systems that support design, production, and item creation. Led a team of 10 in providing production support and played a key role as a DevOps Engineer.
  • CHAT (CC History Analyzer Tool) & DOT (DPO Tool): Developed tools to assist business support teams in analyzing user incidents and tracking product statuses across markets, reducing escalations to the DevOps team.
  • RIO-Duty Drawback: Developed middleware interfaces to manage the duty drawback process for Old Navy Mexico, automating the calculation and reporting of refundable customs duties for returned goods.
  • BP to BAM Integration: Created middleware interfaces to bridge data between Basics Planner and BAM application, automating data extraction and reporting to support weekly planning activities.
  • PAR/AIM Allocation Support: Developed an application to manage the allocation of products to stores across Europe and China for Banana Republic and GAP brands, including both initial and replenishment allocations.

Impact Across Projects:

  • Operational Efficiency: Enhanced support and integration processes, significantly reducing downtime and improving system reliability.
  • Global Consistency: Ensured consistent and accurate data flows across global markets, supporting critical business decisions.
  • User Satisfaction: Delivered intuitive tools and interfaces, improving user experience and reducing incident resolution times.
  • Scalability and Flexibility: Designed and implemented solutions that could scale with GAP Inc.'s growing global operations, ensuring long-term sustainability.
